BuddySystem


Walk safe and make a Buddy along the way!

We are all aware of the age old addage "There is safety in numbers."
BuddySystem is a social betterment app; with the help of BuddySystem you will be able
to meet a walking Buddy and safely walk from place to place and ensure you're not alone.
BuddySystem can appeal to ALL ages and sexes.
Whether you are trying to meet someone who may work at your same company for a regular walking Buddy
to work, a retired socialite looking for company in afternoon walks, or a late night
bar goer looking for a safe walk home a couple of blocks away,
BuddySystem can be used to better your walking experience no matter what walk of life.
