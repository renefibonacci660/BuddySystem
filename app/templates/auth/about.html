About BuddySystem


We are all aware of the age old addage "There is safety in numbers." BuddySystem is a social betterment app; with the help of BuddySystem you will be able to meet a walking Buddy and safely walk from place to place and ensure you're never alone. As a very active individual both personally and professionally who walks everywhere I noticed how much more enjoyable I found my walks from place to place when I was accompanied by a walking buddy and arrived at my desired destination in much higher spirits versus the frequent state of stress and irritation I felt when walking by myself. Although I was initially inspired to create this application out of safety concerns as a woman I recognize that BuddySystem can appeal to all ages and sexes. Whether you are trying to meet someone who may work at your same company for a regular walking Buddy to work, a retired socialite looking for company in afternoon walks or a late night bar goer looking for a safe walk home a couple of blocks away, BuddySystem can be used to better your walking experience no matter what walk of life.
